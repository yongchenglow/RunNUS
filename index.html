About RunNUS

RunNUS is an annual running event organized by NUS Students' Sports Club
to promote healthy lifestyles among the NUS population and the public.

Since its inception in 2007, RunNUS has become one of the biggest and most anticipated event for NUS students, staff and alumni. Above running and fun, RunNUS will be partnering with NUS Disability Support Office (DSO) and inviting Social Service Organizations (SSOs) this year. With the tagline 'Run For A Cause', we aim to empower the community of People With Disabilities/Special Needs (PWDSNs) through inclusiveness.

Race Details

3 Distances, Infinite Possibilities

3 km

Patron

Buddy up with a person with Disabilities/ Special Needs where you will be motivating and assisting them or challenge yourself by experiencing Disability Simulations.

10 km

Challenger

Challenge yourself by navigating through the hilly terrains of NUS. This is the perfect distance for all beginner to intermediate runners and supporters.

15 km

Enthusiast

A route only for those who are brave or are truely believers in the support of persons with Disabilities/ Special Needs. We salute you for your participation.
